God Cares About YOU!

God truly cares about you, you specifically too. His love is like no other and He only wishes to share it with you.

God the Father, God the Son, and God the Spirit are all three different forms of the one true living God. God has always existed and has no begginning. He will forever exist and has no end. God in each form has had all of eternity to think about you. Before the galaxies, the stars, the Earth and the moon, before the angels, the demons, the mountains and the oceans, before even time itself was ever created, God thought about you.

He thought about you often. He envisioned the life you would live, the choices you would make, and the potential you have. He thought about you long and carefully, holding each thought captive for eternity. He could hardly wait to see your smile, to hear your laugh, to see your stumbles, and all of the accomplishments you would make. He dreamed of seeing the people you would help, the things you would create, the memories you would make, how you would call out to Him, and how you would love. God thought about you.
