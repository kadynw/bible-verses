God Cares About YOU!

God truly cares about you. His love is like no other and He only wishes to share it with you.

God has always existed and has no beginning. He will forever exist and has no end. God has had all of eternity to think about you. Before the galaxies, the stars, the Earth and the moon. Before the angels, the demons, the mountains and the oceans. Before even time itself was ever created, you were an idea in the mind of God. God thought about you.

He thought about you often. He envisioned the life you would live, the choices you would make, and the potential you have. He thought about you long and carefully, holding each thought captive for eternity. He could hardly wait to see your smile, to hear your laugh, to see your stumbles, and all of the accomplishments you would make. He dreamed of seeing the people you would help, the things you would create, the memories you would make, how you would call out to Him, and how you would love. God thought about you.

God knows everything about you from beginning to end, and He is so excited. He is so in love with you. He thought about the way you would think, the fire in your eyes, the passion you feel, and the love that you hold within you. God saw every moment of yours in His mind, every jow, every sorrow, every high point, and every low. He played it over and over in His mind, never leaving you and always loving you. God thought about you.

As the Bible says in in John 3:16, "For God so loved the world that He gave His one and only Son, that whoever believes in Him shall not perish, but have everlasting life." We are all sinners and we all fall short of the glory of God, but that doesn't keep Him from loving us so. Truly, He loved us so much that He sent His ONLY Son to live a sinless life, the life we could never live, for us. This is Jesus. Jesus lived as fully God and fully man on Earth to help us in our journey. Jesus was sent to Earth to pave the way for us to grow closer to God and to redeem us of our sins. Jesus died the death that He never deserved for us. He did nothing wrong, yet died the worst death known to man as if He did everything wrong. He died for us. Please give your life to Jesus and read His Word, the Bible. Jesus is God and He loves you so very, very much. God planned your redemption before the universe was ever created and can save you. Truly I tell you, God thought about you.

Please, think about God.

I hope you have a great, fulfilling day and peace be with you always.
